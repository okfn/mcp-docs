# A domain glossary

Energy data is full of terms that mean nothing to a non-expert. To help,
we took many of the official BEN definitions and made them available for
anyone to consult. For people who are not domain experts, this turned out
to be essential.

We went one step further: we injected these definitions into the
**context of the tools**, so the AI itself always has them in mind when
composing each answer, not just the user.

## The effort, and why it is worth it

It takes some work to decide which terms apply to which tool and which do
not. That mapping is manual and a little tedious. But the payoff, clearer
answers and fewer misunderstandings for non-experts, makes it worth the
effort.

If you are building a plugin for a specialised domain, budget time for a
glossary. It is not decoration; it measurably improves the answers.
